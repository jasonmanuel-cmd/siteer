# SiteER Zero-Budget Marketing Plan

## Goal

Use the free scan to generate attention, the `$20` audit to get the first paid conversions, and the fix quote flow to close higher-value work.

## Fastest Channels

1. Direct outreach to local businesses with weak websites
2. Founder posts on LinkedIn and Facebook
3. Posts in Bakersfield and Kern County business groups
4. Google Business Profile posts from the COAI account
5. Referral asks from current contacts

## 14-Day Sprint

### Week 1

- Build a list of 30 local businesses with weak websites
- Run SiteER on each site and save the worst 10 results
- Send 10 personalized emails or DMs per day
- Publish 2 social posts
- Ask 5 trusted contacts for introductions

### Week 2

- Repeat 10 new outreaches per weekday
- Send 5 short teardown videos
- Post 2 more proof-based social posts
- Follow up with everyone who clicked or replied
